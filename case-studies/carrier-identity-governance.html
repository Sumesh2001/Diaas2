Custom Identity Governance Dashboard

Enhance your SailPoint IdentityIQ with custom orchestration to streamline access certification and reduce approval fatigue

Solution Type

SailPoint Extension

Use Case

Any Industry

Category

Identity Governance

Platform

SailPoint IIQ

Overview

Our Custom Identity Governance Dashboard is a powerful extension designed to enhance your existing SailPoint IdentityIQ deployment. This solution introduces additional functionality that significantly improves the end-user experience during Access Certification campaigns without compromising security or compliance.

The solution focuses on streamlining certification workflows, orangeucing approval fatigue, and maintaining audit readiness while ensuring seamless integration with your existing SailPoint ecosystem. Available for organizations across all industries looking to optimize their identity governance processes.

Key Highlights

Custom orchestration layer integrated with SailPoint IIQ

Bulk approval capabilities for unchanged entitlements

Enterprise SSO for secure reviewer access

Scalable architecture for future ISC migration

Challenges

Limited Out-of-the-Box Features

SailPoint IIQ's standard capabilities may not fully align with your organization's vision for an enhanced user experience and streamlined certification process.

Approval Fatigue & Rubberstamping

Reviewers faced overwhelming certification campaigns, leading to approval fatigue and increased risk of rubberstamping without proper review.

Secure Login Requirements

Need for robust, enterprise-grade secure authentication for reviewers and managers accessing the certification platform.

Granular Review Requirements

Requirement for bulk approval of unchanged entitlements while enforcing individual, careful review for modified or new identities.

Our Solution

1

Custom Orchestration Layer

A custom orchestration layer integrated with SailPoint IdentityIQ, designed to extend capabilities while maintaining compatibility with existing workflows and reporting systems.

2

Smart Bulk Approval

Intelligent bulk approval and revocation capabilities for unchanged entitlements, significantly reducing certification review effort and decision fatigue.

3

Enterprise SSO Integration

Secure authentication for reviewers and managers using enterprise Single Sign-On to provide seamless and controlled platform access.

4

MVP Release & Integration

Delivered an externally hosted MVP integrated with IdentityIQ, designed to support future enhancements including RBAC expansion and migration toward SailPoint ISC.
