Access Governance Strategy & Implementation

Strategic roadmap and implementation of comprehensive Identity Governance & Administration framework for a large government organization.

Industry

Government

Location

India

Duration

12 Months

Team Size

6 Consultants

Project Overview

A government organization managing critical national infrastructure needed to establish a robust Identity Governance & Administration (IGA) framework to ensure compliance, reduce security risks, and streamline access management across 200+ applications.

The engagement involved developing a multi-year IGA strategy, selecting appropriate technologies, and implementing the initial phase of access governance capabilities.

Key Highlights

3-year strategic roadmap developed

200+ applications assessed

Automated access certification

75% reduction in access review time

Challenges

Fragmented Access Landscape

No centralized view of user access across 200+ applications with different authentication mechanisms and access models.

Manual Compliance Processes

Time-consuming manual access reviews taking 6+ months to complete, with poor documentation and audit trails.

Regulatory Pressure

Increasing regulatory requirements for access governance and audit readiness with limited resources and expertise.

Our Approach

1

Current State Assessment

Comprehensive inventory of all applications and access patterns, risk assessment of critical applications, analysis of existing access review processes and pain points, and regulatory compliance requirements mapping.

2

Strategic Roadmap Development

3-year phased implementation roadmap, technology selection and architecture design (SailPoint IdentityIQ), governance framework design with roles and responsibilities, and business case and ROI analysis.

3

Phase 1: Foundation Implementation

SailPoint IdentityIQ deployment and configuration, integration with 50 priority applications, identity data aggregation and correlation, and automated access certification workflows.

4

Role-Based Access Control Design

Business role mining and definition for critical applications, entitlement catalog creation and management, Segregation of Duties (SoD) policy definition, and access request and approval workflows.

5

Compliance & Reporting

Automated quarterly access certification campaigns, audit-ready compliance reports and dashboards, SoD violation detection and remediation, and policy enforcement and exception management.

6

Change Management & Training

Stakeholder engagement and communication plan, role-based training for administrators and end users, process documentation and standard operating procedures, and Center of Excellence (CoE) establishment.
