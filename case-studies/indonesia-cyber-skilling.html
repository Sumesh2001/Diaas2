Cyber Workforce Skilling Hub

Building national cybersecurity capabilities through comprehensive IAM training for government ministries in Indonesia.

Client

Government Ministry

Location

Indonesia

Program

6-Week Training

Participants

85 Trained

Overview

In response to Indonesia's national initiative to strengthen cybersecurity capabilities across government ministries, we were selected to establish and operate a comprehensive Cyber Workforce Skilling Hub. The program aimed to build a robust pipeline of cybersecurity professionals with specialized expertise in Identity and Access Management (IAM).

The initiative addressed the critical shortage of skilled cybersecurity professionals in the public sector, focusing on both fresh graduates seeking to enter the field and working professionals looking to reskill for cybersecurity roles.

Program Highlights

National-level cyber skilling hub in Singhasari SEZ

Intensive 6-week classroom training program

Specialized focus on IAM technologies and practices

Career enablement for government cybersecurity roles

Challenges

Structured Training Requirements

Need for a comprehensive, well-structured program capable of effectively skilling and reskilling participants from diverse backgrounds in specialized IAM and cybersecurity domains.

Limited Awareness & Resources

Government ministries faced limited awareness about IAM best practices and specialized training resources, requiring comprehensive educational content and hands-on learning approaches.

Our Solution

1

Cyber Skilling Hub Establishment

Established a dedicated cyber workforce skilling hub at Singhasari Special Economic Zone (SEZ) in Indonesia, equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and learning resources for hands-on IAM training.

2

6-Week Training Program

Developed and delivered an intensive 6-week classroom program covering IAM fundamentals, best practices, tools, and real-world implementation scenarios to bridge theory with practical application.

3

Dual-Track Skilling Approach

Implemented a dual-track program for both fresh graduates (skilling) and working professionals (reskilling), enabling 85 participants to gain specialized IAM expertise for government ministry roles.

4

Career Enablement & Certification

Provided comprehensive career guidance and certification preparation, enabling participants to embark on successful cybersecurity careers with IAM skills aligned to government ministry requirements.

Practical Applications
