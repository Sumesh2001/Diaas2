Case Study

Enterprise IAM Implementation for Global Financial Institution

How we transformed identity management and reduced security risks by 70% through comprehensive IAM implementation

Industry: Financial Services
Duration: 12 Months
Team Size: 8 Consultants

The Challenge

A global financial institution with over 50,000 employees across 30 countries was facing significant challenges with their legacy identity management system. The organization struggled with:

  • Manual user provisioning taking 5-7 days per request
  • No centralized access governance leading to compliance risks
  • Orphaned accounts and excessive privileged access
  • Multiple authentication systems creating poor user experience

Our Solution

We designed and implemented a comprehensive IAM solution leveraging SailPoint IdentityIQ and Okta SSO, following a phased approach:

Phase 1: Assessment & Design

  • • Comprehensive audit of existing identity landscape
  • • Role-based access control (RBAC) framework design
  • • Integration architecture for 200+ applications

Phase 2: Implementation

  • • SailPoint IdentityIQ deployment for identity governance
  • • Okta SSO integration across all major applications
  • • Automated lifecycle management workflows
  • • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) rollout

Phase 3: Migration & Training

  • • Phased migration of 50,000 user identities
  • • Comprehensive training for IT and end-users
  • • 24/7 support during transition period

Compliance Achievement

  • Single sign-on across 200+ applications with 99.9% uptime
  • Achieved SOX, PCI-DSS, and ISO 27001 compliance
